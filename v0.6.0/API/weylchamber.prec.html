weylchamber.prec module¶

Summary¶

Reference¶

weylchamber.prec.DEFAULT_WEYL_PRECISSION = 8¶

Precision to which to calculate Weyl chamber coordinates or local invariants.

Making the precision too high can lead to numerical instabilities, as rounding errors can put a point outside of the Weyl chamber when it should be inside, or into a different region.
